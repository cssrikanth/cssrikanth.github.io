I mainly work on developing discrete adjoint solvers for steady and unsteady fluid flows which will be used in aerodynamic optimisations. I have considerable experience in developing finite volume and meshfree based CFD solvers. I am the main developer of the meshfree solver, MFCFD. Check here to follow the code progress.

I mainly use FORTRAN 90 to develop my codes but I am open to explore possibilities in developing codes with modern languages like Julia and Python especially from an HPC point of view.
